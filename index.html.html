Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast easy version of the classic, Chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking time Total time
Easy10 min25 min35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together.

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour.

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough.

4

Bake in the oven.
